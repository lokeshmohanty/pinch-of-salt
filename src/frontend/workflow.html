System Workflow

How we process news into a verifiable timeline.

1

RSS Ingestion

The system fetches live feeds from diverse global sources (BBC, NYT, Al Jazeera) using feedparser. We collect titles, descriptions, and publication dates to build a broad base of information.

2

Semantic Clustering

Using scikit-learn, we vectorize article text (TF-IDF) and apply DBSCAN clustering. Articles with a cosine similarity > 0.3 are grouped into a single "News Cluster", ensuring we identify different reports of the same event.

3

LLM Extraction & Classification

Clusters are passed to the Llama-3.2 model. It synthesizes a unified title, extracts verified facts, classifies the geography/category, and identifies links to previous events in our database.

4

DAG Generation

The system stores temporal relationships (parent/child) between events. The Knowledge Graph visualizes these clusters as a Directed Acyclic Graph, showing the historical flow and consequences of events over time.
